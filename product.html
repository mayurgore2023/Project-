Coconut oil:


Krisi-Organic'sCoconut oil is an Unprocessed, Unrefined, Pure, Chemical free oil extracted using traditional Bull driven Ghana/Chekku. We do not subject oil into any processing to retain maximum nutrients. Bull driven Ghana rotates at 2-3 rotations per minute, crushing seeds at ambient temperature without generating heat. Nutri 360 edible oils are free from chemical solvents and free radicals generated due to heat from machine Ghanas and refineries. Consuming wholesome oil extracted from traditional bull driven Ghana/Chekku will improve your overall health.
