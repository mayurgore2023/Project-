Krishi-Organic's



No chemical is used while extracting the oil, especially no machine filter process is done because it requires heat above smoke (boiling) point. We perform manual filter process which settles down the oil for 12 hours. Thus it is thick as compared to refined oil and it is full of natural values due to which taste, colour and smell of the oil remains same like farm fresh, also cooking time is decreased.
We provide pure and organic wooden ghan which was initially invented by the ancient sages of India. Instead of animals we use electric motor to churn out the organic wooden ghan. We use finest quality of exported raw material. Our main aim is to provide organic wooden ghan so that our customer stay healthy and fit.
